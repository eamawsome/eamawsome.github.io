I wake up as my bed vibrates and makes annoying sounds. I groan but get up and see Lili; our robot trying to tell me something. “Your mom bleeped you, she wants you to finish your Christmas shopping. She also wants you to bring Nicole to do her shopping.”

“Ok I’ll try,” I said.

So now I have to wake up Nicole. I never knew they programmed robots to try and get the easy way. Well, I guess they are just trying to make it more humanoid. That’s why they look like humans. And talk like humans. Just WHY do they have to be so ANNOYING, I think as I go to wake up Nicole. As I enter her room her bed vibrates.

Go away! Why can’t we just order it on amazon?”, Nicole groans.

“Mom said it was to expensive.”, I lied.

“Fiiiiiine.”

“Yeah and you haven’t been bleeping me either”, I say as I throw a snowball at him.

“Well let's at least get together sometime.”

“OK sounds cool!”

“Come on let's go Nicole!”, I say.

“Hey I can help you with Nicole, in fact my brother is right over here!”

“That would be great Aaron, Thanks!”

Inside the mall Nicole marvels at every little thing. “Can I get that?”

“I’m bored”, Owen whines.

“Why don’t we go to Orange? I know Owen likes to “test” out the games”, Aaron says excitedly.

Well I hope they don’t break anything cus’ I’m not paying for it! What would my mom like? Maybe I can give her some gift card? No! I can give her the free rooms for The Water Discus Hotel I won at school! I was trying to tell her but this is the perfect gift!
